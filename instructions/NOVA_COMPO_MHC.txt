Description
Nova Compo MHC is a light–curing, radiopaque, micro–hybrid universal composite for anterior and posterior restorations. It cures with light in the wavelength range of 400–500 nm (blue light) with a suitable device.
Composition
The monomer matrix is composed of hydrophobic aromatic dimethacrylates, Bis-GMA, UDMA, PEGDMA, small amount of TEGDMA (<% 5). The ﬁllers contain barium alumunium borosilicate (Mean 0.6µm), ytterbium, (20-50 nm) nano silica, silica-zirconia and prepolymer as a organic filler (80 % by weight and more than 57% by volume), additives, catalysts, stabilizers and pigments.

Indications
Restorations of deciduous teeth
Splinting of mobile teeth
Restorations in the posterior region (classes I and II)
Anterior restorations (classes III, IV)
Class V restorations (cervical caries, root erosion, wedge–shaped defects)
Extended ﬁssure sealing in molars and premolars
Veneering of discoloured anterior teeth and Repair of composite and ceramic veneers
Contraindication
If a dry working ﬁeld cannot be established, or if the stipulated technique cannot be applied.
If a patient is known to be allergic to any of the ingredients in Nova Compo MHC.
Application
Isolation: Appropriate isolation, best with a rubber dam.
Cavity preparation: Cavity preparation is carried out according to the requirements of the adhesive technique, i.e. protecting the tooth structure. Do not prepare sharp,internal edges or additional undercuts in caries–free areas. The dimensions of the cavity are generally determined by the extent of the caries or the size of the old restoration. Bevel enamel edges in the anterior region. For the posterior region, only the sharp enamel edges should be rounded (ﬁnishing diamonds). Caries–free cervical defects are not prepared, only  cleaned with pumice or other suitable cleaning pastes with the help of rubber cups or rotary brushes. Subsequently, remove all residue in the cavity with water spray and dry with water– and oil–free air.
Pulp protection / Base: Do not apply a base material when using an enamel/dentin bonding agent. Only cover very deep areas close to the pulp with a calcium hydroxide material .Do not cover other cavity walls, since they can be used to support the bond with an enamel/dentin adhesive.
Matrix / Interdental wedge: Use a wraparound matrix for cavities affecting the proximal area or a sectional matrix and wedge it.
Application of the bonding agent: Condition and apply the bonding agent according to the Instructions for Use of the product in use.
Application of Nova Compo MHC Composite: For an optimum result, apply in layers of max. 2 mm or 1.5 mm. If you are using 500 mW/cm2 light device, light cure 20 seconds, with LED 10 sec.per 2 mm layer for enamel, transparent colours and light cure 20 seconds per 1.5 mm layer for dentin colours. Use a special contact point instrument for large cavities where the contacts are difficult to create.
When using a metal matrix, additionally polymerize from the buccal or lingual/palatal after removing the matrix. In many cases, a ﬂowable composite is used today as an initial layer to create an even cavity ﬂoor and to facilitate the adaptation of the subsequently used restorative material. A ﬂowable can be used in a thin initial layer. This optional layer has to be cured separately (please refer to the respective Instructions for Use).
Finishing and Polishing: Remove excess material with suitable ﬁnishers or ﬁne diamonds after polymerization. Remove proximal excess with diamond or tungsten carbide ﬁnishers, ﬁnishing strips, or ﬂexible ﬁnishing discs. Check the occlusion and articulation and apply appropriate grinding corrections to prevent premature contacts or undesired articulation paths on the surface of the restorations. Use silicone polishers as well as polishing discs and polishing strips to polish the restorations to a durable high gloss.
Cautions
Unpolymerized Nova Compo MHC should not come in contact with skin, mucous membrane, or eyes. Unpolymerized Nova Compo MHC may have a slight irritating effect and may lead to a sensitization against methacrylates. Commercial medical gloves do not provide protection against the sensitizing effect of methacrylates.
Storage
Do not use Nova Compo MHC after the indicated date of expiration. Storage temperature 1–30 0C / 39.2–77 0F. Close syringes immediately after usage. Exposure to light causes premature polymerization.

Uygulanmayan Alanlar (Kontrendikasyonlar)
Nova Compo MHC bileşenlerine kaşı kanıtlanmış alerji olması durumunda kullanılmaz.
Uygulama
İzolasyon: Tercihen rubber dam kullanarak kuru bir çalışma ortamı için izolasyon yapılır.
Kavite Preparasyonu: Adeziv teknik kurallarına uygun olarak kavite hazırlanır. Kavite su spreyi ile temizlenir. Hava sıkılarak kurutulur. Eğer bir mine/dentin bonding ajanı kullanıyorsanız, bir baz materyal uygulamayın.
Pulpanın Korunması: Pulpaya yakın çok derin bölgeleri bir kalsiyum hidroksit materyaliyle örtün.
Matriks Yerleştirilmesi: Matriks yerleştirilir.
Bonding Uygulanması: Kullanılan ürünün kullanım talimatlarına uygun olarak bonding uygulanır.
Nova Compo MHC Uygulanması: Nova Compo MHC maksimum 2 mm veya 1,5 mm kalınlıktaki tabakalar halinde (dentin renklerinde, bleach XL’de) uygulayın. 500 mW/cm2 şiddetinde ışık cihazı ile her tabakayı 20 sn ,LED ışık cihazları ile 10 saniye boyunca polimerize edin. Işık cihazını restoratif materyalin yüzeyine mümkün olduğunca yakın tutun. Bir metal matriks kullanıyorsanız matriksi çıkardıktan sonra ayrıca bukkal veya lingual/palatal yönlerden polimerize edin.
Uygun bitirme ajanları veya ince taneli elmas frezlerle işlemi tamamlayın. Oklüzyon kontrol edilir. Silikon cilalayıcılarla, parlatma diskleri veya polisaj bantları kullanın.
Saklama koşulları
Saklama sıcaklığı 1–30 0C. Şırıngalar kullanıldıktan sonra hemen kapatılmalıdır. Işığa maruz kalması istenmeyen zamansız ön polimerizasyona neden olur.
Nova Compo MHC son kullanma tarihinden sonra kullanmayın. Enjektörleri oksitleyici dezenfeksiyon maddeleriyle dezenfekte etmeyin.
Uyarılar
Polimerize olmamış Nova Compo MHC’ nin cilt, mukoza ve gözlerle temas etmesinden kaçınılmalıdır. Nova Compo MHC sertleşmemiş haldeyken hafif irrite edici etkiye sahip olabilir ve metakrilata karşı duyarlılığa neden olabilir.
Piyasada bulunan cerrahi eldivenler metakrilatlara karşı duyarlılık için koruma sağlamayabilir.
